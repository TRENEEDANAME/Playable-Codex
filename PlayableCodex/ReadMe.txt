# Playable Codex [WOTC]

Description = Allows you to have a Codex on your side. You will get them as a project in the proving grounds. Need a new campaign.

## Cost

- Rookie =  1 elerium core, 1 codex corpse, 300 supplies
- Veteran = 2 core, 2 brain , 350 supplies
- Commander = 3 core, 3 brain , 400 supplies
- Legend = 5 core, 5 brain , 500 supplies

Every codex ability works (psi bomb, cloning, teleport...)

You can also use the "AddCodexSquaddie" command

## Required mod and DLC

Required DLC = WOTC
Required mod = none
